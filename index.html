What We Do

At Lowe’s India, we are a powerhouse in the retail industry, driven by innovation and excellence. Our expertise spans across Enterprise Architecture, Data Science & Analytics, Omni-channel Platforms, Financial planning & Analytics, Supply Chain, Merchandising, Marketing, and beyond. With a holistic approach, we deliver exceptional customer experiences, optimize business processes, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving retail landscape.

Our diverse capabilities enable us to create a unified shopping experience, curate a wide range of products and drive brand awareness and loyalties through creative strategies. At Lowe’s India, we are committed to continuous innovation and delivering outstanding results in all aspects of our operations.
